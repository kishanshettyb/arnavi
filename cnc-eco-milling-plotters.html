CNC Eco Milling Plotters

We produce BLACKBIRD CNC milling plotters in the range of 500x700mm to 2100x2500mm work areas. The working range of the Z axis is 150mm or 200mm. It is a proven design containing many innovative solutions that we have developed based on many years of experience. Machines of this series are addressed primarily to advertising companies, prototype workshops, producers of packaging, punching dies, and service companies. All BLACKBIRD machines are equipped with a control system with dynamic vector analysis, smooth regulation of the feed speed and spindle revolutions. The guides, ball screws and optical limit sensors are fully covered, which ensures minimum maintenance related to the cleaning and maintenance of the machine.

BlackBird plotters are available in several versions. The most frequently chosen machine from this group is the device with the equipment presented in the photo. With 24,000 rpm spindle without automatic tool change and T-slot table.

T-slot tables made of anodized aluminum profiles greatly facilitate the fastening of materials. The second option is a vacuum table, which is perfect for mounting large sheets of cardboard, paper, plastic, wood-based panels, aluminum sheets and others. HDF boards are used as an intermediate layer between the table and the material. They are ideal for this purpose as they are air permeable, so the material lying on top of them is attached. You can cut elements through without worrying about damaging the table. When they get damaged, they can be planned or replaced with new ones, the advantage is that they are cheap and easily available.

An alternative to the spindle without replacement is the spindle with automatic tool change plus a tool magazine, it will improve and shorten the processing time that requires the use of many tools.

Instead of a spindle, you can use an active head with an oscillating knife and a creasing wheel. Perfect for working with paper, cardboard, cardboard, rubber, leather, fabrics and other soft materials. Thanks to it, we can make prototypes of packaging before making a die, cut small and medium series of gaskets, trial patterns for shoes, etc. The cutting-creasing head can also be ordered as removable additional equipment installed on the spindle.
