CNC milling router

Milling plotters of the BLACKBIRD CNC series are produced in the range of working areas 500x700mm to 2100x2500mm. The working range of the Z axis is 150mm or 200mm. It is a proven construction containing many innovative solutions that we have developed on the basis of many years of experience. Machines of this series are addressed primarily to advertising companies, prototyping plants, manufacturers of packaging, punching dies, service plants. All machines of the BLACKBIRD series are equipped with a control system with dynamic vector analysis, smooth regulation of feed speed and spindle rotation. The guides, ball screws and optical limit sensors have been fully shielded, ensuring a minimum of maintenance for cleaning and maintenance of the machine.

BlackBird plotters come in several versions. The most frequently chosen machine from this group is a device with the equipment presented in the photo. With 24000 rpm spindle without automatic tool change and T-groove table.

T-groove tables made of anodized aluminum profiles greatly facilitate the attachment of materials. The second variant is a vacuum table, which is perfect for fixing large sheets of cardboard, paper, plastic boards, wood-based, aluminum sheets and others. As an intermediate layer between the table and the material, HDF boards are used. They are ideal for this purpose, because they let air through, so the material lying on them is attached. You can cut elements inside out without worrying about damage to the table. When they are destroyed, they can be planned or replaced with new ones, the advantage is that they are cheap and easily available.

An alternative to a non-replaceable spindle is a spindle with automatic tool change plus a tool magazine, which will streamline and reduce the machining time that requires the use of many tools.

Instead of a spindle, you can use an active head with an oscillating knife and a crease. Ideal for working in paper, cardboard, cardboard, rubber, leather, fabrics and other soft materials. Thanks to it, we can make prototypes of packaging before making a die, cut small and medium-sized series of gaskets, test blanks for shoes, etc. The cutting and creasing head can also be ordered as a removable accessory mounted on the spindle.
