Dental Engravers

HighSpeed ​​CNC engravers

Table engraving machine in the High Speed version with a parallel configuration with a movable table. The closed design makes the machine quieter and easy to keep clean, and the small size will allow it to be placed in office rooms. Ideal for engraving in wax, laminates, plexiglass, plastics. It is also great for aluminum and non-ferrous metals, when making hot-stamping dies and stamps. By combining parallel kinematics technology with an excellent control system with dynamic vector analysis, we have achieved performances that are many times higher than the applicable standards. The electrospindle with a maximum speed of 24,000 rpm and the tool length correction system allow for extremely efficient work while maintaining excellent machining quality.

5-axis HighSpeed ​​CNC engravers

Five-axis table engraving. The machine, aimed primarily at prosthetists and goldsmiths, allows you to make five-axis models of wax and zirconium oxides. Fully enclosed, it is perfect for clean office spaces. The total body also significantly reduces noise from the spindle. By combining parallel kinematics technology with an excellent control system with dynamic vector analysis, we have achieved performances that are many times higher than the applicable standards. A high-end induction electrospindle with a maximum speed of 60,000 rpm and automatic tool change with a magazine allow for extremely efficient work while maintaining excellent machining quality.
