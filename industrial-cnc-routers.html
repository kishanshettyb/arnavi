Industrial CNC Routers

The world's first serially produced cutter on linear drives opens up new production possibilities in many industries. The Kimla BPT Linear cutting plotter is available in a variety of work areas and equipment options. The compact structure of the machine has been equipped with innovative solutions based on linear drives, which have been designed and adapted especially for this device.

New generation cutter

A new cutter with linear drives and a completely new design allows to achieve even higher efficiency and quality of work. High-performance cutting is possible thanks to the use of direct position measuring systems with ultra-high accuracy - measurement resolution 0.001 μm and positioning repeatability of 1 μm. The linear drives used in this machine are virtually maintenance free as the magnetic drive does not cause any friction, so nothing wears out, eliminating costly repairs over the life of the machine.

Dedicated control

Our proprietary control system with dynamic vector analysis ™ has been specially designed for this device in order to maximize the potential of our linear drives. From now on, even very complex shapes can be processed with unprecedented efficiency and precision. The built-in all-in-one system contains all the necessary CAD / CAM / CNC / NESTING modules, which ensures high comfort and precision of machining.

Processed materials

The Kimla BPT Linear cutting plotter offers a very wide range of processed materials thanks to the possibility of extending it with many additional options. Perfect for cutting: leather, fabrics, fibers, composite, gasket, layered materials, paper, cardboard, foil.

Additional options The

Konwejor is a belt table that automatically moves the material, which increases the comfort and efficiency of work. The convejor enables continuous operation, which reduces unnecessary downtime of the machine. In addition, the machine can be equipped with a receiving and loading table, targeting system, oscillating heads, milling heads, writing heads, marking heads, creasing heads.
