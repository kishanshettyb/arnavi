Who We Are

Exclusive sales and technical service partner Across India Region

Arnavi Machines Pvt Ltd - “Arnavi” is a brain child of two mechanical engineers who has in-depth Knowledge of Sales, Marketing, Service & Customer Success. We have more than 20 years of expertise in providing customized market solution to business leaders. We have vast experience of CNC machine installation and service across the globe and have supported more than 300 machines to achieve the desired goal. We understand the Key factors that are driving the CNC machines market growth include growing demand for higher productivity and reduction in downtime and increasing demand for mass production from the industrial sector. So we are collaborated with the best in class European brand KIMLA for their world class product Sales & Services across India.

KIMLA - As they pride themselves on being one of the leaders in the industry, having installed more than 3500 machines to customers' companies globally, they are constantly developing and seeking new solutions to customer demands. KIMLA machines achieve industry-leading performance at prices which are significantly lower than those offered by reputable companies elsewhere in the world.
